Mapping New Survey Sites in Aurora, Philippines using QGIS

In this project, I utilized QGIS to create a detailed map of new survey sites in Aurora, Philippines. The objective was to visually represent the newly surveyed areas while incorporating regional administrative boundaries and topographical information to provide a comprehensive understanding of the landscape. Key components of the project: Cartographic Skills: I applied advanced cartographic techniques to design a clear and visually appealing map. The map emphasizes the newly surveyed sites while maintaining a professional and accessible layout. Administrative Data: I incorporated administrative data to accurately display regional boundaries, helping users understand the geographical context of the survey sites within Aurora. Digital Elevation Model (DEM): I integrated a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) to highlight the terrain's elevation, offering a 3D perspective of the region's topography. This was particularly useful for understanding the landscape's influence on the survey areas. This map provides a valuable tool for decision-makers, enabling them to assess the newly surveyed sites in the context of regional boundaries and elevation, all presented with professional cartographic design.

  • QGIS
  • Shapefiles
  • Raster data
